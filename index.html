Flocking Boids

How it works:

  • Move your mouse to move the food.
  • ANGRY fish will become red and chase the target, either the food under cursor or a pink fish.
  • Fish will become ANGRY if they collide more than 50 times.
  • ANGRY fish will be happy if they either got their target, or forget after 7 seconds.
  • When an ANGRY fish ate the food (cursor), it becomes pink, and another random fish will become ANGRY, and start to chase this pink fish.

Train and Bezier Curves

How it works:

  • Click checkbox RUN to start/pause the train.
  • Drag the red dots on the rail to move and modify rail shape.
  • "SHIFT+Click" to add a point to rails (will add to end of the rail dots list).
  • "CTRL+Click" on a red dot will remove it.
  • You can change number of carts, toggle simple track, toggle arc-length parameterization in the control section below.
